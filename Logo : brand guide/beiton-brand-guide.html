Design principles

1. Restraint over decoration

Every element must earn its place. No decorative gradients, no unnecessary icons, no visual noise. The brand communicates trust through white space, precise typography, and a monochromatic palette.

2. Content-first hierarchy

The most important information appears first and largest. Prices, services, and actions are never buried. The chat interface is the hero — the first thing a visitor sees and interacts with.

3. Transparent pricing

All prices come from official legal sources (Notaries Regulations). The price calculator is prominent and honest. No hidden fees, no "call for pricing" — prices are displayed clearly.

4. Multilingual by default

Every page exists in 6 languages. Language detection is automatic. The experience is equally polished in Hebrew, English, Russian, Arabic, French, and Spanish. RTL and LTR layouts are first-class citizens.

5. Technology as trust

The AI chat, instant pricing, and real-time order tracking signal modernity and efficiency. Technology is visible but not flashy — it simply works.
